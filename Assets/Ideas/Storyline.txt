Prince Kuber was born to King Teren and Queen Tasila, at the time of Great War.
He grew up in the world of Artemos.

As a young boy, he had one friend Vared that spend most of the time with it. 
They loved to play with swords and acted like they were in combat for real.
He also had a horse that was same age as him. When he was not with his friend Vared, he would spend the time riding his horse and playing with him.
Kuber was trained in combat by Megadit and later became a skilled swordsman, while at the same time learned the arts of self control.
